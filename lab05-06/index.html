Our Staff

Dr. Harry Jennings

Harry graduated from the Ontario Veterinary College in 2001 and, after a few years in the industry, opened Hintonburg Animal Hospital in 2006. He remains the sole owner of HAH. He loves hiking in the Gatineau hills and spending time with his Great Dane, Hunter.

Dr. Kristi Russell

Kristi mentored under Dr. Jennings after graduating from the Ontario Veterinary College in 2010 and has been with us ever since! She loves baking and hanging out at home with her two cats, Dexter and Amity.

Dr. Cecelia Flores

Cecelia was born in Madrid, Spain in 1982, and came to Canada in 2000 to study at the University of Toronto. She graduated in 2007 from the Western College of Veterinary Medicine in Saskatoon and has been at HAH since 2012. She's also our resident bird expert!

About us

We are a small clinic located in the heart of beautiful Hintonburg, in Ottawa, Canada. Our dedicated staff has proudly served the Ottawa pet community for over 15 years.

Our Services

We offer inpatient and outpatient medical treatment for eye, ear, skin, gastrointestinal, respiratory, cardiac, urogenital, renal and hepatic diseases. Prices vary by animal needs.

We provide surgical services ranging from routine spays and neuters to more complex bladder, ear, eye, gastrointestinal, orthopedic and tumour removal procedures. Prices vary by operation.

In addition to our medical and surgical services, we are fully equipped with the latest in dental equipment, including digital dental radiography. We offer routine cleanings (prophylaxis) ($300) and dental extractions (prices vary).

Our veterinarians care for your pets with the latest in technology, including a state-of-the-art digital radiograph machine which allows for speedy results. $50 - $250.

Let us give your pet a healthy start with a new puppy or new kitten examination. We are experienced in pediatric medicine and love seeing new pups and kittens.
